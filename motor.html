How Hydrogen Motors Work

Hydrogen motors typically refer to fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEVs), which convert hydrogen gas into electricity to power electric motors.

Key Components:

  • Fuel Cell Stack: Converts hydrogen and oxygen into electricity.
  • Hydrogen Tank: Stores hydrogen gas at high pressure.
  • Electric Motor: Drives the wheels.
